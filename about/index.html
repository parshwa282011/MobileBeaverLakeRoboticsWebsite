History of Club

Our beloved supervisor Mr. Kevin Christensen started Robotics Club in _____. Part of Issaquah school district middle schools, Beaver Lake Middle School Robotics have maximum FLL and FTC teams. This club was even active and running during COVID time as well and teams participated in remote events. This club is funded by Beaver Lake Middle school, and donations from parents.
