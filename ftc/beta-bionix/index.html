Beta Bionix


Beta Bionix is a First Tech Challenge (FTC) team from Beaver Lake Middle School (BLMS). Our mission is to build the best robot, running the most efficient software that competes amongst the best teams in FTC every year.


This year we participated in the Spencer League Meet and Tesla Interleague Tournament where we won the Innovate Award (2nd Place) and were the second pick of the Finalist Alliance. In the Washington Tech Invitational (WTI), we were the second pick of the Semi-Finalist Alliance.

Outreach

Our team believes in being strong ambassadors for the FIRST program and our outreach activities have focused on spreading awareness of FIRST. We aimed to bring more of our community into FIRST and STEM through fundraisers at our school, demoing the robot, and informing groups such as the Girl Scouts on the benefits of being part of the FIRST family. Currently, we are working with a girls orphanage in India to spread FIRST and STEM awareness We also worked with local community service oriented organizations such as Kiwanis and KidVantage to give back to the community. ​



Our Outreach also included connecting and engaging with STEM industry professionals. These connections were made possible as a result of our parent mentors. Through these events we have gained knowledge on technical aspects, robotics in the industry, as well as the future of AI and robots.​
