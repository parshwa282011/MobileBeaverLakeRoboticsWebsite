FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC)

FTC stands for FIRST Tech Challenge in which we build our robots from metal peices and some 3D printed parts to try to make our robot score the most amount of points in a match with another team as a alince against two other teams


In 2023-2024 season, both teams participated Spencer League Meets and Tesla Interleague Tournament where both teams were selected for playoffs. Alpha Intelligence was the first pick of the Semi-Finalist alliance, while Beta Bionix was the second pick of the finallist alliance. This year none of the teams were able to advance to states but in Interleagues both Alpha and Beta were doing much better and improved a lot from League Meet 1 and 2. In our last comp of the season - WTI both teams were selected for Playoffs and Aloha Intellignence was the second pick of the winning alliance.


In the 2022-2023 season, both teams participated in Spencer League Meets and Tesla InterLeague Tournament where they were both on the same Alliance, which won Tesla Interleague. In Interleagues Beta Bionix was selected for the Inspire Award and Alpha Intelligence was selected for the Control Award and was the Winning Alliance Captian with Beta Bionix and 8693 Scarbs. In another compotetion called Washington Tech Invitational both teams were slected for playoffs and Alpha Intelligence was one of the 4 Captians. While Beta Bionix was the first pick of the finallist alliance first pick. Then both teams went to states to try and qualify for Worlds in Houston. Both teams did very good and they were both on the winning alliance but they were not able to move on to Worlds.
