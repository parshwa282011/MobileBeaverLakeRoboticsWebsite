Beaver Lake Robotics Club

Welcome to Beaver Lake Robotics Club. Our club consists of three FLL (First Lego League) teams and two FTC (First Tech Challange) teams. Any members from Robotics club can join either of these teams based on available spots in teams. FLL teams have up to max 10 members and FTC teams have up to 15 members. FLL teams mainly have 6th and 7th graders while FTC teams will have 7th and 8th graders.
